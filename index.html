Action & Stewardship

Activities that heal you and the earth

Turn your visit into a contribution. Step By Park offers purposeful recreation where your personal wellness routine directly supports the sustainability and regeneration of our shared green space.

Mindful Nature Journaling

Slow down to sketch or note local flora. This practice sharpens mental focus and reduces anxiety while helping us monitor biodiversity changes without disturbing habitats.

The "Plogging" Loop

Combine jogging with litter collection. This eco-fitness trend boosts your endorphins and heart health while keeping our trails plastic-free and safe for local wildlife.

Canopy Care

Join our tree-tending sessions. Working with soil is proven to lower cortisol levels, and your physical effort ensures our young trees survive to filter the city air.

Native Seed Gathering

Connect with neighbors during harvest season to collect native seeds. It fights social isolation and provides the essential stock needed to rewild future urban zones.
