Litter Lunges

Legs & Glutes 50 Cal

The Mission: Spot a piece of litter? Turn it into a fitness rep.

  • Stand feet hip-width apart. Step forward into a deep lunge.
  • Keep back straight. Pick up litter at the bottom of the movement.
  • Push back to start position. Dispose responsibly.
Mark Complete

Hydration Haul

Arms 30 Cal

The Mission: Carry watering cans with "Farmer's Walk" form.

  • Deadlift cans safely (straight back).
  • Walk with shoulders retracted and core tight.
  • Perform a bicep curl before pouring water on plants.
Mark Complete

Path Sweeper

Cardio 100 Cal

The Mission: High intensity sweeping intervals.

  • Sweep vigorously for 45 seconds to clear path.
  • Rest or walk for 15 seconds.
  • Repeat for 10 minutes. Switch leading arm halfway.
Mark Complete

Compost Diggers

Shoulder & Core 55 Cal

The Mission: Turn compost or organic waste into a workout.

  • Hold shovel firmly.
  • Dig into compost section.
  • Turn & mix materials.
Mark Complete

Trim & Tone

Arms 25 Cal

The Mission: Trim small safe plants and shrubs.

  • Hold clippers at mid-height.
  • Snip excess leaves slowly.
  • Avoid sharp thorns and stems.
Mark Complete

Mulch Movers

Arms 45 Cal

The Mission: Spread mulch with full body movement.

  • Scoop mulch safely.
  • Spread evenly around plants.
  • Step back to smooth surface.

Weed Warriors

Arms 30 Cal

The Mission: Remove weeds carefully like a fitess rep.

  • Spot weed at soil level.
