Find Peace

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday. For urgent matters outside these hours, you can email us at contact@stepbypark.org and we'll respond as soon as possible.

How long does it take to get a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For technical support requests, we aim to provide an initial response within 4 hours during business hours.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes, we offer phone support for all customers. You can reach us at +959 785 008 911during business hours. For faster service, please have your account information ready when you call.

Can I schedule an in-person meeting?

Absolutely! We welcome scheduled meetings at our San Francisco headquarters. Please contact our office at +959 795 214 810 or email contact@stepbypark.org to schedule a time that works for you.

What information should I include in my support request?

To help us assist you faster, please include: your name, contact information, account number (if applicable), a detailed description of your issue, and any error messages you've encountered. For technical issues, screenshots are very helpful.
