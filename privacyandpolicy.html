Privacy Practices & Policy

Overview & Commitment

This Privacy Policy describes how ParkRevive Inc. ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information through our website and services dedicated to revitalizing local parks and supporting personal well-being.

Your privacy and security are our top priorities as we foster connections between green spaces and personal growth.

  • We are transparent about the data we collect and how we use it.
  • We only use your data to improve your experience and community impact.
  • You control your information and its visibility at all times.
  • We actively protect and never sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

To support revitalization projects and enhance your well-being connection with parks, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: such as name, email, age (13+ only), and location (optional or user-selected).
  • Usage Data: how you interact with our platform, including visited park pages, features used, and participation in revitalization projects.
  • Contribution Content: posts, photos, event participation, well-being journal entries (optional), and community discussions.
  • Device & Technical Data: browser type, IP address, device type, and certain cookies for functionality and analytics.
Why We Collect This Info
We use this data to help you track your well-being journey, see your positive impact on parks, and build community connections!

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide, personalize, and improve our Services for a better well-being experience.
  • Organize and facilitate park revitalization projects and challenges.
  • Aggregate community data (never individually identified) for research and community reports.
  • Communicate upcoming park events, platform updates, and wellness opportunities (you may opt out anytime).
  • Maintain platform safety and prevent abuse related to park content and projects.
Your Data Is Safe
We do not sell your data. Your individually identifiable data stays private unless you expressly share it with the community.

Sharing & Disclosure

Your information may be shared only as follows:

  • With Your Consent: If you join public park projects or discussions, content you contribute may be viewable by other users.
  • Service Providers: For essential services like web hosting, analytics, and email (bound by confidentiality obligations).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with laws, government requests, or protect safety and rights.
  • Aggregated Analytics: Non-identifiable summary data may be published to encourage park revitalization (e.g., "200 trees planted this month by community").
Important: We never sell your personal information or share it for commercial marketing.

Data Security & Retention

  • Encryption: Sensitive personal and contribution data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Access Control: Only essential personnel may access user data, and only for platform improvement or support.
  • Retention Policy: We retain your information only as long as it is needed for park revitalization or well-being purposes, or as required by law.
Data Anonymization
Your well-being and activity data may be anonymized and pooled to help improve parks and share community success stories, never tied to your name.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your profile and shared content at any time through your account settings.
  • Request correction or restriction of data processing by contacting us directly.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications (e.g., newsletters or event invites).
  • Request data export or deletion in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
Controlling your Data
You are always in charge. Contact us for data requests or to learn how your information is used for park and well-being programs.

Policy Updates & Contact

  • We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes.
  • Your continued use of our platform following updates means you accept these changes.
  • For questions, requests, or feedback regarding this policy or your privacy, email us at privacy@parkrevive.com.
Questions? We’re here to help you make the most of your park and well-being journey!
